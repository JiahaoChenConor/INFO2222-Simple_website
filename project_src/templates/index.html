Time to Study Web!

Have a dream to become a web developer? Or just feel bored at weekends?
Welcome to our website and start you journey of studying web development!

Why choose our website?

There are thorough materials for you to learn: how the web works, HTML/CSS, Javascript
and everything you may need. Just a freshman or already have some knowledge about the web? Both are fine!
You can just take a simple test and let we know the current knowledge you have, so that you can start
at a certain level of difficulty - basic, intermidiate or advanced. To ensure you enjoy the study,
some youtube videos are added and these also enable you to have a better understanding of the concepts.
Need practices? No worries! Visit the challenge module and get exercises as much as you want.
Feel alone when studying? Our tutors and other users are waiting for you at the discussion forum! Post your
questions there and feel free to communicate with others!
